Favorite Morning Drink

I love a good cup of Milk Tea

Here is my Recipe for my cup of Milk Tea

  1. Firstly it varies based on what I have with me. I usually use boiling water and tea bags (usually 2 tea bags minimum for stronger flavor) to simmer my tea. In Bangladesh, people usually simmer the tea leaves in a boiling pot with boiling water but I mainly use tea bags for convenience
  2. I then use either condensed milk or creamer. I sometimes used dehydrated milk and just milk in general but then I would do a optional third step
  3. This step is optional in case your milk does not have a sweetner, in this case, I either use sugar or honey
  4. Now we stir everything and wait 10 minutes, after which we enjoyed after freshly made morning cup of tea :)
